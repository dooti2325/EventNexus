Tree Plantation Drive

Date: 5th June 2024
Time: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Venue: Campus Green Area

Description:

An initiative to celebrate World Environment Day by planting trees around the campus. Join us to contribute to a greener future and make a positive environmental impact. This event is a great opportunity for students, faculty, and staff to come together in support of sustainability.

Activities:

  • Tree planting in designated campus areas
  • Educational session on the importance of trees
  • Distribution of saplings to participants

Participation Details:

Eligibility: Open to all students, faculty, and staff.

Entry Fee: Free

Maximum Participants: 100

Event Type: Group Activity

Special Guest:

A guest speaker from an environmental organization will join to share insights on climate change and sustainability.
